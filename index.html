About

In my chess persuit, a partner said I would excel at web development. Both chess and web development are based on logic. Building a good app is like playing a chess game, it requires forethought, organization, and sometimes it may seem like little progress is being made but that's likely part of one of the most crucial steps! I want to develop because both chess and web development start with determination and have enjoyable payoffs the more one becomes skilled.

Along with chess, I have enjoyed a similar field that shares skills with web development. I have taken classes in three languages (Spanish, French, and Italian). In fact, I lived in France with my family for four months in 2000. Being immersed in culture and language in France is similar to being a student at General Assembly. Every bit of language I acquired, both in France and GA, was put to use and was really appreciated because it enabled me to make new pathways and I saw the results immediately. It opened new windows to unforseen experiences and I plan to continue this pattern here in Boston or Chicago.

Commercial Killer

Commercial Killer is a user powered commercial detection app. It is essentially Ad Block Plus for one's television. It uses javascript, ruby on rails, and PSQL to function. The app has particular front end features like giphs, point counters, twitter modals, and badges. This developer created Commercial Killer in under a week. Hopefully one day you can see it in the app store!

Bucket Buddy

Bucket Buddy is an app created in a four person group at General Assembly. It has features like profiles, authentication, and social features like seeing all users' buckets. A bucket is a bucket list item that a user creates and the app keeps track of one's bucket list goals. Bucket Buddy uses express, javascript, and MongoDB for front and back end as well as server capabilities, respectively.
